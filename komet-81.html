Komet 81 â€“ High-Performance Bulk Packaging Solution

The Packit Komet 81 Form & Seal Bagmaking Machine by VS International is an advanced intermittent motion system designed for bulk packaging, delivering speeds of up to 40 bags per minute. It efficiently handles 5 kg to 10 kg packaging with precision and reliability.

Powered by three servo motors, it supports various packaging styles, including center seal pillow packs, side gusset bags, flat bottom pouches, D-cut, and Euro slot packs, with a width range of 280mm to 840mm. A durable vertical sealing system ensures strong and secure seals, while an automated film handling mechanism enhances efficiency.

A sensor-based roll monitoring system alerts operators when the roll is near completion, reducing downtime. Its intelligent motion control system ensures smooth operation, and the user-friendly design allows quick laminate replacement. The machine integrates seamlessly with cup fillers, auger fillers, and multihead weighers, offering flexibility for various products.

With additional eye mark sensors, nitrogen flushing, coding systems, and dust collection units, the Komet 81 delivers speed, accuracy, and efficiency, ensuring cost-effective and seamless operations backed by expert support.
