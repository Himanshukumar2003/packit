Servo Auger Filler - High Precision Powder Filling Solution

The Packit Servo Auger Filler by VS International is an advanced packaging solution designed for precise and efficient dosing of fine powders. Engineered for high accuracy, it ensures consistent filling performance, making it ideal for industries requiring reliability and speed.

Equipped with a servo-driven, brushless, direct-drive system, the machine delivers exceptional precision and durability. It features high-quality mechanical components, including servo motors, encoders, and material-level sensors sourced from reputed international vendors, ensuring smooth and efficient operations.

Designed for versatility, the Servo Auger Filler efficiently handles products such as grounded spices, flour, and all types of fine powders. The robust construction allows for minimal maintenance, easy adjustments, and long-term reliability.
