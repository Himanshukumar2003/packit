TPI 912 Duplex Cup – High-Speed Packaging Solution

The Packit TPI 912 Duplex Cup by VS International is a high-speed packaging machine designed for precise and efficient dosing of granules and free-flowing products. Built for duplex operations, it delivers a reliable speed of up to 120 packs per minute, making it ideal for industries requiring fast and accurate packaging.

Equipped with a dual-cup dosing system, the TPI 912 ensures consistent and uniform filling, minimizing wastage. Its automated cup adjustment mechanism allows seamless volume modifications, catering to various product needs. A robust cup filling mechanism ensures precise measurement and controlled dispensing, while an intelligent synchronization system optimizes performance.

Designed for convenience, it allows quick adjustments and easy maintenance, reducing downtime. It integrates seamlessly with multiple packaging formats and includes enhancements like an eye mark sensor, nitrogen flushing, vibratory feeding, coding, static charge eliminator, and a take-away conveyor.

Part of VS International’s Packit series, the TPI 912 Duplex Cup guarantees efficiency, accuracy, and seamless operations.
